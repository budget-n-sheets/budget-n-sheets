Enter a passphrase to protect your budget spreadsheet backup.

Passphrase Requirements

  • At least 12 characters long
  • At least 1 lowercase letter
  • At least 1 uppercase letter
  • At least 1 number
  • At least 1 symbol
    The allowed symbols are:
    ~ ! @ # $ % ^ * - _ = + [ { ] } / ; : , . ?

If you lose your passphrase, you will not be able to restore the backup.
