Bill Calendar

Visit the wiki to learn how to setup and sync your financial calendar: Wiki - Bill Calendar.

Before playing the examples below, open the Settings sidebar, select a calendar and check the option "Sync calendar to the spreadsheet".

Create calendar events and update the cash flow play_circle_outline

  • The simplest event: This simple event has the name of an account and a number formatted.
  • Muted event: This event has the "@muted" indicator, so it is not included in cash flow, nor posted in the table.
  • Pay day: Similar to the The simplest event but with a few tags. While the tags don't play any role in cash flow, they are posted in the table along with the other details.
  • Card bill payment: This event has the "#qcc" built-in tag and no number formatted. The add-on gets the card's balance of the previous month and uses it in the cash flow.
  • Two day events: Multiple-days events behave like one-day event.
