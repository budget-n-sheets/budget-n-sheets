Back up data & settings

You can back up data and settings from your budget spreadsheet to your Google Drive. You can restore your backed-up information to a new budget spreadsheet. The size of the backup varies by spreadsheet and settings.
What gets backed up
  • Jan, ..., Dec transactions
  • Cards transactions
  • Tags
  • Accounts and Cards
  • Add-on settings

You need a password to encrypt your backup.

The developers of the add-on Budget n Sheets, as well as the add-on Budget n Sheets itself, have no knowledge of, way to retrieve, or way to reset your password.

Don't lose your password! Budget n Sheets won't be able to reset it or recover your backup data if you do.

An email will be sent to you with the backup file attached to it. You can save the attachment to your Google Drive.
